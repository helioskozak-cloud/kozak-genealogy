What Betsy's Research Confirms (Sources S49–S55)

Ancestry.com records shared by Betsy Relihan Gross document a Derby CT family:

  • Confirmed: Peter George McGivney (b.Feb 7 1891, Derby CT) married Helen R. Relihan (b.Jan 12 1894) on June 14, 1921 in Derby CT. No children. Both buried Mt. St. Peter's Cemetery, Derby CT.
  • Helen's parents: Maurice Relihan (b.1856 County Kerry, Ireland; d.Aug 2 1921 Shelton CT) + Mary Dolan (b.1866 County Roscommon; d.1911 Derby CT).
  • Peter George's parents: Peter McGivney (b.1844 County Cavan, Ireland; d.Sep 15 1898 Derby CT, gardener & florist) + Ann McGivney née Clark (b.1867 County Cavan; d.Feb 13 1948 Derby CT).

Key conclusion: This McGivney family (County Cavan, Derby CT) is almost certainly a separate Irish-American family from Blessed Michael J. McGivney (whose father Patrick came from County Cork, Waterbury CT). The Betsy records confirm a Relihan–McGivney marriage but not to the Knights of Columbus founder's branch.

Open question: Is Maurice Relihan (County Kerry, Shelton CT) related to Grant's Relihan ancestors (John Relihan Sr. + Ann Duley, Bridgeport CT)? If yes, Grant has a documented family connection to a Connecticut McGivney line — though still not to Blessed Michael's branch.
