## Research Summary

### What Was Proven

1. **Michael G. Demchak — full biography confirmed (S39).** Michael G. Demchak (b. October 12, 1924, Bridgeport CT; d. February 4, 2019, Masonicare of Wallingford, age 94) is the brother of Mary Demchak Shomsky. Parents: George M. Demchak Sr. + Helen (Kosch) Demchak. Graduate of Harding High School, Bridgeport. US Army WWII veteran (Asiatic-Pacific Theater, Philippine Liberation Ribbon, Good Conduct Medal, Victory Medal, Connecticut Veterans Wartime Service Medal). Career: General Electric Bridgeport, then US Baird Machinery Corporation Stratford. Member St. John's Carpatho Russian Orthodox Church, Bridgeport. Avid bowler and golfer; member North End Bethany Senior Center. Source: Adzima Funeral Home, Stratford CT.

2. **Michael G. Demchak's wife: Mary Figula Demchak (S42).** Mary F. Demchak (b. Bačkov, Czechoslovakia, to Michael + Mary (Malik) Figula; d. October 23, 2016, Masonicare of Wallingford, age 88). Long-time Bridgeport resident. Married Michael G. Demchak ~1948, married 68 years. Communicant of St. John's Carpatho Russian Orthodox Church, Bridgeport — member Seniors Club and Mother's and Daughter's Club. Children: Gary Demchak + wife Cynthia (Meriden CT); Darlene Gallagher + husband Dennis (Monroe CT). Grandchildren: Christina Demchak, Gregory Demchak. Source: Adzima Funeral Home, Stratford CT.

3. **Ann Demchak Vayser — full biography confirmed (S40).** Ann Demchak Vayser (b. August 21, 1921, Bridgeport CT; d. April 10, 2012, Milford Health Center, age 90). Widow of Joseph H. Vayser Jr. Born to George M. Demchak Sr. + Helen (Kosch) Demchak. Retired secretary, Bridgeport Brass Company. Member Seniors of St. John's Carpatho-Russian Orthodox Church, Bridgeport. Funeral: Adzima Funeral Home, Stratford CT; Requiem Service at Carpatho-Russian Orthodox Church of St. John the Baptist, Bridgeport; interment St. John's Cemetery, Stratford. Siblings at death: Michael Demchak (surviving, Bridgeport); Mary Shomsky (predeceased), Helen Karaffa (predeceased), George Demchak (predeceased). "Several nieces and nephews." Ann's children and grandchildren: NOT listed in obituary. Source: Adzima Funeral Home (S40).
